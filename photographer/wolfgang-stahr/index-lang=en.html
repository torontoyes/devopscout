Wolfgang Stahr studied photography at the university of applied science in Bielefeld, Germany and graduated in 1996. His work is focused on portrait, people, interior and travel mostly for international magazines and ad-clients. Amongst that he also works on projects in international galleries and museums. His work has been awarded many times, including ADC, Lead-Awards and the Grand Prix Européen de la Ville de Vevey.

He speaks German, English and French (basic). His projects took him to all five continents and he thinks of himself for being curious, resilient, good-humored and flexible. Photography is his passion and he is proud and thankful to be able make his passion his job.

Clients include: Aktion Mensch, Allianz, Bundesministerium für Arbeit und Soziales, Deutsche Post, Hornbach, Mercedes Benz, SPD, Telekom, Vattenfall, AD-Architectural Digest, BMW Magazin, Die Zeit, Harper‘s Bazaar, Monopol, Vogue, a.o.
